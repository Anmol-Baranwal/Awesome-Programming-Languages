- **Creator** - Larry Wall
- **Release Date** - 1987
- **Links** - [Official Website](https://www.perl.org/), [GitHub Repo](https://github.com/Perl/perl5)
- **Applications**
  * Text Manipulation
  * System Administration
  * Networking
  * Databases
  * Embeded Systems
